# Godot Download Guide
## Step 1
### Download Godot Engine
[Godot Engine download link](https://godotengine.org/download/windows/)

![Godot Engine Web Page](./Guide%20Images/godot_engine_website.png)

# Step 2
### Un-zip Godot Engine file

![Godot Engine Zip File](./Guide%20Images/zip_file.png)

## Step 3
### Save this unzipped folder in a safe place where it won't be accidently deleted or lost

## Step 4
### Open the unzipped folder and double press *Godot_v4.7-stable_win64.exe* to open the Godot Engine

![Open Engine](./Guide%20Images/exe_files.png)

## Step 5 (Optional)
### Right click *Godot_v4.7-stable_win64.exe*, and press "Pin to taskbar" or "Create shortcut" for later use

![Create shortcut image](./Guide%20Images/shortcut.png)
